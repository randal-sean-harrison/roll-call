Meaningful mentorship means knowing your students

Foster deeper educational connections. Use this app to learn your student's names while taking attendance.

How this works ...

Visit ND Student Photos website to download any course roster to a familiar location, such as your desktop.

If you need help downloading the roster, review these steps. If you like, you can explore the process with this sample data.this sample data.

Now, click "Choose File" to upload the course roster (PDF) you just downloaded. We'll extract student names and photos from that file.
